About

Hearthstone is a collectible card game by Blizzard Entertainment.
This tournament will be held online. After signing up, players will be assigned opponents to play against by the tournament administration.

Tournament Rules

Tournament Style:
The tournament will be held in a
Single-Elimination Best-of-Three style.
The finals will be a Conquest-Style Single-Elimination


Rules:
Best of Three: Any decks can be used.
Failing to submit match results on time without prior notice will result in disqualification.


Conquest Finals:
Each player must send us decklists of three unique decks from three different character classes.
A player caught modifying a deck at any point in the finals will be disqualified. (See Conquest Explanation)


Streaming:
The finals will be streamed on Twitch.tv and posted to YouTube.

Time and Dates:

Tournament Match Recording:
After the two players finish their match, they must contact the tournament administration and submit the results of their match. Both players must submit a screenshot of their match result to us by 11:00 PM of the day they are assigned to play, unless otherwise told. All players will be notified of their position in the tournament and brackets will be updated daily by 11:59pm.
